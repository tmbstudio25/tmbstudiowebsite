Who We Are

Bringing Local to Global

TMB Studio is a Malaysian indie game and animation studio dedicated to creating immersive experiences through game development, 3D animation, and motion graphics.

Inspired by the myths, folklore, and untold stories of Southeast Asia, we transform cultural heritage into modern interactive experiences that entertain, inspire, and leave a lasting impression.

Driven by creativity and a passion for storytelling, we continue to explore new ideas, refine our craft, and share our journey as we build original worlds for players and audiences around the globe.
